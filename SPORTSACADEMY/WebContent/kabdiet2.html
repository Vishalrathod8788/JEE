Kabaddi Player Diet Plan

Meal Food Options (India)
Breakfast
  • Option 1: Upma (semolina) with vegetables and a glass of buttermilk
  • Option 2: Paratha (stuffed flatbread) with curd and pickle
Mid-Morning Snack Fresh fruit (e.g., banana or apple), mixed nuts (almonds, walnuts)
Lunch
  • Option 1: Rice with chicken curry, mixed vegetable sabzi (curry), and salad
  • Option 2: Roti (whole wheat flatbread) with dal (lentils), paneer (cottage cheese) curry, and curd
Afternoon Snack Chana chaat (chickpea salad) with cucumber, onion, and spices
Pre-Training Snack Whole-grain bread sandwich with chicken or tofu and veggies
During Training/Match Stay hydrated with water or a sports drink with electrolytes
Post-Training/Match
  • Option 1: Protein shake (whey or plant-based), rice, fish or tofu, and saut√©ed greens
  • Option 2: Lassi (yogurt-based drink) with a protein-rich snack like sprouts or boiled eggs
Dinner
  • Option 1: Roti (whole wheat flatbread) with fish curry, brown rice, and mixed vegetable sabzi
  • Option 2: Bajra (pearl millet) roti with aloo (potato) sabzi and curd
Evening Snack Yogurt with honey and a handful of dry fruits (e.g., almonds and cashews)

General Tips:

  • Focus on locally available and easily understandable foods.
  • Include a mix of carbohydrates, proteins, and healthy fats.
  • Minimize sugary and processed foods.
  • Maintain balanced meals.
  • Pay attention to portion sizes.
  • Stay well-hydrated with water or homemade beverages.
  • Allow time for digestion before intense training or matches.
