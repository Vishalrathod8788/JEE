    Welcome to Our Sports Academy


    We are a dedicated sports academy committed to helping athletes of all levels achieve their full potential. With state-of-the-art facilities and experienced coaches, we provide top-notch training in various sports disciplines.

    Whether you're a beginner looking to learn the basics or an advanced athlete aiming for excellence, our academy is the perfect place for you to develop your skills and achieve your sports goals.


    -----VISION-----


    RSA envisions to turn India into next sports super power by focusing on developing the young athletes holistically. For this, RSA has embedded the need of the athlete at the core and aims to provide the rising athlete with quality infrastructure, equipment and training. Along with professional sports training, RSA guides the youth towards developing a methodology to live a healthy, fit and disciplined life throughout.



    -----MISSION-----

      To raise a Rising Athlete by:

    • Recognizing the uniqueness of each player.
    • Strengthening the strong points and skills of the player.
    • Evaluating the performance and betterment day by day.
    • Beating the weakness within.
    • Achieving the highest of the highs.

    -----HOW WE DO IT?-----

    • By never stop learning so as to never stop growing!
    • Encouraging not only the young athlete but also our professional masters to keep learning
      the best techniques and practices from the ever growing world of sports.
    • Ensuring a three-way communication through regular interaction with parents for supporting the young
      athlete to reach to their full potential and give their best.
    • Giving equal importance to physical, mental and emotional fitness of the player
      with the blend of both ancient practice (such as yoga, meditation for focus and concentration) and modern physical education.
    • Maintaining a positive surrounding and frequently inviting achievers to inspire young players.
    • Understanding the need of each individual player and utilizing 21st century skills and technology to
      prepare the athlete to create an opportunity for themselves instead of waiting for it.
