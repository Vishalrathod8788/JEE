Volleyball Player Diet Plan

Meal Food Options (India)
Breakfast
  • Option 1: Idli (steamed rice cakes) with sambar and coconut chutney
  • Option 2: Whole-grain cereal with milk, sliced fruits, and nuts
Mid-Morning Snack Yogurt with honey and fresh seasonal fruits
Lunch
  • Option 1: Roti (whole wheat flatbread) with chicken or tofu, brown rice, mixed vegetable sabzi (curry), and salad
  • Option 2: Rice with dal (lentils), aloo (potato) sabzi, and curd
Afternoon Snack Carrot and cucumber sticks with hummus
Pre-Training Snack Banana or apple, whole-grain bread sandwich with chicken or paneer (cottage cheese) and veggies
During Training/Match Stay hydrated with water or a sports drink with electrolytes
Post-Training/Match
  • Option 1: Protein shake (whey or plant-based), quinoa or sweet potato, grilled fish or tofu, and saut√©ed greens
  • Option 2: Buttermilk with a protein-rich snack like sprouts or boiled eggs
Dinner
  • Option 1: Roti (whole wheat flatbread) with fish curry, brown rice, and mixed vegetable sabzi
  • Option 2: Bajra (pearl millet) roti with palak paneer (spinach and cottage cheese) and curd
Evening Snack Mixed nuts (almonds, walnuts) and a cup of herbal tea

General Tips:

  • Focus on locally available and easily understandable foods.
  • Include a balance of carbohydrates, proteins, and healthy fats.
  • Minimize sugary and processed foods.
  • Maintain balanced meals.
  • Pay attention to portion sizes.
  • Stay well-hydrated with water or homemade beverages.
  • Allow time for digestion before intense training or matches.
