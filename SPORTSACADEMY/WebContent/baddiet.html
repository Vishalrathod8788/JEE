Badminton Player Diet Plan

Meal Food Options
Breakfast Omelette with whole-grain bread, fresh fruit (e.g., banana or apple)
Mid-Morning Snack Mixed nuts (almonds, walnuts), Greek yogurt with honey, carrot sticks
Lunch Roti (whole wheat flatbread) with chickpea curry, mixed vegetables
Afternoon Snack Whole-grain crackers with hummus, cucumber and tomato slices
Pre-Training Snack Banana, peanut butter on whole-grain bread
During Training/Match Stay hydrated with water or electrolyte drink
Post-Training/Match Protein shake (whey or plant-based), veggie and paneer (cottage cheese) wrap
Dinner Lentil dal (pulses), brown rice, mixed greens salad
Evening Snack Curd (yogurt) with berries or sliced papaya, herbal tea

General Tips:

  • Focus on locally available and easily understandable foods.
  • Prioritize whole grains, lentils, vegetables, and fruits.
  • Minimize processed and sugary foods.
  • Maintain balanced meals.
  • Pay attention to portion sizes.
  • Ensure proper hydration with water or electrolyte drinks.
